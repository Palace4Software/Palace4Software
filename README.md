[Palace4Software on CodeBerg](https://codeberg.org/Palace4Software)

🌐 **Our Official Website:**

## ℹ️ Why CodeBerg?
We chose CodeBerg as our primary public mirror because it aligns better with our [values of **Free & OpenSource ideology**](https://palace4software.eu/our-vision). CodeBerg's commitment to providing a free and open platform for hosting and sharing Free & OpenSource Software projects resonates with our own philosophy.

We use [SquashBot](https://palace4software.eu/squashbot/) to polish our public GIT mirrors:

- **Squashes commits per release** – one clean commit per version tag
- **Keeps things tidy** – the public sees thoughtfully prepared releases

The workflow is:

**Internal repo → SquashBot → CodeBerg (public mirror)**

This keeps our internal GIT history separate from what you see publicly, while making sure our projects are easily accessible.
