# 👋 Welcome to Palace4Software

All active development happens *in our internal infrastructure*. In addition to our website, the source code of our Free/Libre & OpenSource projects is published via CodeBerg, where our internal repositorys are processed by SquashBot and published as cleaned mirrors.

👉 **Public mirror:**
[Palace4Software on CodeBerg](https://codeberg.org/Palace4Software)

🌐 **Our Official Website:**
[Visit Palace4Software](https://palace4software.eu)

## ℹ️ Why CodeBerg?
We chose CodeBerg as our primary public mirror, because it aligns better with our [values of **Free/Libre & OpenSource ideology**](https://palace4software.eu/our-vision), than other platforms with similar features. CodeBerg's commitment to providing a free and open platform for hosting and sharing Free/Libre & OpenSource Software projects resonates with our own philosophy.

We use [SquashBot](https://palace4software.eu/squashbot/) to polish our public GIT mirrors:

- **Squashes commits per release** – one clean commit per version tag
- **Keeps things tidy** – the public sees thoughtfully prepared releases
